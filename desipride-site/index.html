Desi Pride: Elevating Indian Craftsmanship

Desi Pride is a premium Indian lifestyle and spirits company committed to celebrating the bold, diverse, and authentic spirit of India. As a parent company to trailblazing brands such as "EXAMPLES", Desi Pride curates and crafts high-quality alcohol and tobacco products that fuse traditional Indian heritage with contemporary taste.

Rooted in local ingredients, iconic aesthetics, and regional craftsmanship, Desi Pride is more than a company—it’s a movement to reclaim the richness of Indian indulgence. Whether it’s a heritage-infused liquor or a homegrown cigar wrapped in nostalgia, every Desi Pride product tells a story of subcontinental flair, cultural pride, and fearless innovation.

With a vision to redefine Indian luxury on its own terms, Desi Pride serves discerning consumers in India and beyond, offering them a taste of the unapologetically desi—with elegance, mischief, and unmistakable style.
